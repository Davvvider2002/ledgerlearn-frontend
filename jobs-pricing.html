Common questions
Do I need a card for the free trial?
No. Your 30-day trial starts the moment you register — no card, no commitment. You'll be prompted to upgrade when the trial ends.
What happens after my trial expires?
Your account stays open and your applications are preserved. Your active listings are paused until you upgrade. Candidates cannot apply to paused listings.
What is the $39 per-post charge?
A one-time charge per job listing. Not a subscription — you are charged once and the listing stays live for 30 days. Pay again to renew or post a new role.
Can I verify applicant certificates?
Yes. Every applicant's LedgerLearn certificate ID links to our public verification page at ledgerlearn.pro/verify — you can confirm it instantly.
How does the monthly plan work?
Pay $149 via PayPal subscription. Unlimited postings for the billing period. Cancel any time — your listings stay live until the period ends.
What regions do candidates come from?
Nigeria, South Africa, UK, UAE, Australia, Ghana, Kenya and more. You can filter your listing to a specific region or accept global applicants.

Questions about pricing or bulk hiring? Email hello@ledgerlearn.pro for a custom arrangement.
