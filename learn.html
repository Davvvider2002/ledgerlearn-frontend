📋 Step-by-step: creating a new invoice in Xero
1
Navigate to Accounts → Sales, then click Invoices in the left panel.
2
Click the blue "+ New Invoice" button (pulsing above). Opens a blank invoice with your default template.
3
In the To field, type a contact name. Xero autocompletes from saved contacts. Type + Enter to create a new one.
4
Set Invoice Date and Due Date. Due dates follow payment terms set in your contact settings (e.g. 30 days net).
5
Add line items: Description, Qty, Unit Price, Account Code (e.g. 200 for Sales), and Tax Rate. Totals calculate automatically.
6
Click Save as Draft or Approve → Email directly from Xero. Approved invoices update your accounts receivable in real time.
